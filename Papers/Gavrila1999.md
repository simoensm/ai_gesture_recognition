## Abstract

Comprehensive survey of the state of the art in visual analysis of human motion as of 1999. Covers body tracking, gesture recognition, and action recognition from video. Discusses DTW and edit distance as the dominant sequential comparison methods of the era.

---

## Key Contributions

- Systematic taxonomy of human motion analysis: tracking → recognition → interpretation
- Survey of DTW and edit distance as the dominant tools for gesture comparison
- Discussion of challenges: occlusion, viewpoint, speed variation, clothing
