# Using dynamic time warping to find patterns in time series

> [!info] Metadata
> **Authors:** Donald J. Berndt, James Clifford
> **Year:** 1994
> **Venue:** Proceedings of the 3rd International Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining (KDD Workshop)
> **Citekey:** `Berndt1994`

---

## Abstract

One of the first papers to adapt DTW from speech recognition to general time-series data mining. Berndt & Clifford demonstrate that DTW can discover recurring patterns in financial and medical time series, making the technique accessible beyond speech processing.

---

## Key Contributions

- Transferred DTW from speech recognition into general time-series pattern discovery
- Introduced the idea of using DTW as a similarity measure for subsequence search
- Demonstrated applications in stock market data
