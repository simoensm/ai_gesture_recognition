## Abstract

Comprehensive review of the DTW algorithm, covering basic formulation, constraints (Sakoe-Chiba, Itakura), step patterns, and applications. An excellent practical reference for anyone implementing DTW from scratch.

## Key Sections for This Project

- Formal DTW recurrence with boundary conditions
- Sakoe-Chiba band and Itakura parallelogram constraints
- Symmetric vs asymmetric step patterns
- Complexity analysis
